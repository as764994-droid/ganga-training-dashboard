What is Project GANGA?

Project GANGA — Government Assisted Network for Growth & Advancement — is a Uttar Pradesh Government initiative that aims to connect homes, schools, hospitals, businesses, and government offices across UP with reliable high-speed broadband.

The programme supports aspiring entrepreneurs with interest-free, collateral-free loans under Mukhyamantri Yuva Udyami Yojana, along with complete training, technology support, and hand-holding by ONE Broadband — one of India's leading private ISPs.

Approved applicants become Digital Service Providers (DSPs), delivering high-speed broadband and digital services to their local communities and building a growing monthly income.
